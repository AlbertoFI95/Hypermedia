Our services

Our energies are in action every day. Through associations in the area, our members and our volunteers are engaged daily in networks of relationship, solidarity and participation. About the association

Auser is a voluntary and social promotion association, committed to promoting the active aging of the elderly and enhancing their role in society.
Our association proposal is addressed primarily to the elderly, but is open to dialogue relations between generations, nationalities, different cultures. An association for which the person is the protagonist and resource for himself and for others in all ages.
